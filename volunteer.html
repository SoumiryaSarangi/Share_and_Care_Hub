FAQ's

How does this work?

When you sign up to us you are signing up for a series of emails from Share and care club. Our letters will be short, sweet, and complete with a dash of inspiration.

What can I expect after sign up?

When you sign up you will receive a welcome email from us followed by a few follow-up emails. After that, you will receive our bi-monthly Volunteer Newsletter.

Can i choose which type of volunteering i wantv to do?

We have hundreds of thousands of volunteer opportunities. We encourage you to explore the site based on your interests! There are nearly 10 different cause areas and a range of filters to help you find what you are looking for.

Are the volunteer opportunities where i live?

We have volunteer opportunities in all states! When you search by your local area, you can find opportunities nearest to you.

Are there a fees for these services?

Nope! When you commit to finding a volunteer opportunity, we promise to help you find one. We won’t try to sell you anything—ever!
